ABOUT THE PROJECT

A mobile application developed using Android Studio, Google Maps API, and Firebase Realtime Database to improve emergency reporting in Tondo, Manila. The app enables users to report incidents such as fires, crimes, and accidents and connect them to the responders, with an interactive map for marking locations or manual address input. It includes offline-accessible emergency hotlines and a real-time status tracking system to monitor reports from acknowledgment to resolution.

ROLE

Lead Frontend Developer; Backend Contributor (Mapping & Database)
